Our Story

From Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010, STRIDE began as a small workshop with a big dream - to create footwear that combines unparalleled comfort with cutting-edge style. What started as a passion project between two college friends has grown into a globally recognized brand.

Our first collection featured just three styles, handcrafted in a garage in Portland, Oregon. Today, we operate state-of-the-art facilities where we continue to honor our commitment to quality craftsmanship while embracing sustainable manufacturing practices.

Our Philosophy

At STRIDE, we believe shoes should do more than just look good. They should empower you to move through life with confidence and comfort. That's why every pair we create undergoes rigorous testing to ensure it meets our high standards for durability, support, and style.

We're committed to ethical sourcing and sustainable practices. From the materials we select to the way we package our products, every decision is made with both people and the planet in mind.

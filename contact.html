Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn shoes in their original condition with packaging. Please contact our customer service team to initiate a return and include your order number. Return shipping is the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or our error.

How do I determine my shoe size?

We provide detailed sizing information for each product on its page. We recommend measuring your foot length and comparing it to our size chart. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for comfort. Our customer service team is also happy to provide sizing recommendations based on the specific style you're interested in.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. Duties and taxes may apply depending on your country's import regulations and will be the responsibility of the recipient. Please contact us before ordering if you have specific questions about shipping to your location.

Are your shoes ethically made?

Absolutely. We're committed to ethical manufacturing practices. All our shoes are produced in factories that meet strict labor standards, with fair wages and safe working conditions. We regularly audit our supply chain to ensure compliance with our ethical standards. Many of our materials are also sustainably sourced.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website. If you're having trouble tracking your package or it appears delayed, please contact our customer service team for assistance.
